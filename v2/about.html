The story

Less noise. More control.

a26 Products started with a simple observation: founders and operators don’t actually want “a factory.” They want a product that arrives on time, on spec, and at a cost structure that holds up when they reorder.

But manufacturing is rarely sold that way. It’s sold as access. Access to a country. Access to a supplier list. Access to a contact. And then the hard part begins: aligning specs, managing sampling, negotiating tradeoffs, enforcing timelines, and building quality control that catches issues before they ship.

That hard part is the business. So that’s what we do.
