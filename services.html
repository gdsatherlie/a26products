Service lanes

Engage where you need support — or hand it off end-to-end.

Factory matching & planning

We get aligned on what’s realistic, then identify the right manufacturing partner based on fit — not a random list or a rushed intro.

  • Align on goals, priorities, timeline, and “non-negotiables”
  • Match factories based on capability + reliability
  • Surface risks early (MOQ, lead time, quality expectations)
Typical engagement: 1–2 weeks • Clear plan + factory recommendation

Sampling & production setup

We manage the back-and-forth required to get to a production-ready product — without you chasing details across time zones.

  • Coordinate samples and revisions
  • Lock expectations so production doesn’t drift
  • Prepare packaging / handoff materials as needed
Typical engagement: 2–6 weeks • Samples to production-ready

Production oversight & quality control

Once production starts, we stay involved to keep things moving and catch issues before they become expensive problems.

  • Timeline and milestone oversight
  • Ongoing factory communication + issue resolution
  • Quality checks before shipment release
Typical engagement: Active during production • Ongoing oversight

End-to-end ownership

For teams who want one accountable partner from start to finish. We handle planning, sourcing, sampling, production, and quality under one roof.

  • Single owner across the entire process
  • Clear decision points and documentation
  • Reorder playbook so the second run feels routine
Typical engagement: Launch ownership • One accountable operator

Service fees cover a26’s execution and oversight. Production costs, materials, tooling, freight, duties, testing, and third-party inspections are quoted separately based on your product and suppliers.

Production financing (case-by-case)

For qualified projects, financing may be available to support sampling, production, or initial inventory. This is not guaranteed and is subject to eligibility, underwriting, and third-party approval.

  • Best fit: repeatable products with clear demand signals and realistic margins
  • Use cases: first inventory run, working capital for production, bridging a reorder
  • How it works: we discuss fit on the call and share options if applicable

We’ll always tell you quickly if financing is not a fit — no long drawn-out process.
